Our Story

We're a team of makers.

Our story started with two people who both have four university degrees, traveled all over the world, lived in several countries, have worked for years in and with international companies and have a big circle of expat friends. The idea of our service came up 2 years ago when one of us just came back from traveling through China. Being seated next to a Japanese expat family in the airplane, who were immigrating from Japan to Brussels and very nervous about all the things still needed to be arranged. After talking with them for hours, the seed was planted, we immediately knew there had to be so many expats out there who were feeling helpless and overwhelmed, not only the stress of leaving home and starting somewhere new but most of all not knowing where to start and who to contact.

We conducted our own research within our expat circle and read different (scientifical)articles and research on expats and their needs, what are their biggest nuisances, and most of all what is the main reason why expats often return home earlier than expected. All these interviews, talks, statistics gave us new insides on what expats actually need.

Surprisingly we found out that the main reason why expats often did not feel at home or returned home early, besides the reason of their family not feeling at home, was the lack of local support. Even when you live in a great neigbourhood, when you have found the right job and got the kids in the right schools.

Busy work schedules will quickly dominate, this leaves little time to manage your personal matters or any inconveniences you will encounter especially the first couple of months. Inconveniences such as, you get a letter from the municipality about a certain matter but it is written in Dutch, you need to separate garbage and request for an extra bin, your wife wants to do online groceries but the menu is in Dutch. You need to find glutenfree products for your son but where to go. Your roof is leaking but the plumber does not speak English. You want to book a nice weekend away in Europe with the family but where to search for cheap flights and good accommodation. For any inconveniences or problems you encounter, we are here. For most expats it is a reinsurance to know you always have someone you can call 5 days a week.

We are your local support team when you have no idea where to turn to.
What we can offer you besides our expat services is most of all a smooth and soft landing.
